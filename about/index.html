Isabel is a New York City based actress from Berkeley, CA. While getting her BFA at NYU she studied Commedia dell'Arte in Florence, Irish Theatre in Dublin, Shakespeare at RADA in London, and Brecht in Berlin. Currently in her last semester, she is finishing a minor in Web Programming and Applications (she made this website!) and writing an Honors Thesis about performance artists who use real violence in their work.
